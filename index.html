About Me

G’day, I’m Callan — the bloke behind SSC Electrical. I’ve been in the electrical industry for over 20 years, and in that time I’ve seen it all — from dodgy switchboards to tight ceiling crawls to full-scale solar setups.

I started SSC Electrical with a simple goal: deliver honest, high-quality electrical work that customers can count on. I believe in doing the job properly the first time, treating every site like it’s my own home, and making sure you’re never left in the dark.

Fully licensed and insured, I back my work with experience, attention to detail, and pride in getting it right — safely and cleanly.
